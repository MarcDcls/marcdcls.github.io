Marc Duclusaud

Currently a robotics engineering student at ENSEIRB-MATMECA in Bordeaux, I am passionate about robotics, particularly its applications in the field of prosthetics. This passion, combined with my eagerness to learn more about the subject, is what drives me to pursue a PhD in this field. Before specializing, I followed a curriculum focused on computer science, which allowed me to develop strong skills in this area—skills that I believe will be valuable for my future research. In parallel with my studies, I am also a FabManager at the Bordeaux INP Fablab, where I have the opportunity to work on a wide range of technological projects. This experience allows me to develop skills in various fields, such as mechanics, electronics, and mathematics. It has also given me hands-on experience with numerous tools and machines, including 3D printers, laser cutters, and CNC machines. If you would like to discuss any of the projects featured on this site, feel free to contact me. I am available for any additional information.
